THE REUNION OF A BAND THAT NEVER SPLIT

Welcome back Deja.... oops, you never left! Everyone, most of all myself, will be glad to hear that Deja Norm'al is not only NOT broken up but they are also psyched about playing Before the Fall!
These kats are so amazing and Mike is so on fire for the Lord! I have had the honor of talking to him (via the great phone invention) and was so encouraged by his zeal for the King. He is truly a sold out follower of Jesus and his love is contageous. What really got me going was the fact that he went out of his way to ask and ensure that he would be allowed time to give his testimony and talk about the Lord! Hey bud, all the time you need.... I am just glad you came back, from having never really gone.

Play Hard.... Pray Harder!
